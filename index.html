Project Description

During my internship at Zidio, I was responsible for designing a tech blog website. Following the company's instructions and targets, I utilized Figma to create a visually appealing and user-friendly interface. My design process included:

  • Conducting user research to understand the target audience's needs and preferences.
  • Developing wireframes and prototypes to visualize the website layout and functionality.
  • Iterating on designs based on feedback from Zidio to achieve the final version.

MY APPROACH

I tackled the tech blog website design project using a systematic and organized approach:

  • Target Analysis: Initially, I thoroughly analyzed the target requirements provided by Zidio. Understanding the specific goals and expectations of the project was crucial to ensure that the final design aligned with the company's vision and objectives.

  • Competitive Research: Following the target analysis, I conducted comprehensive research on existing tech blog websites. This step involved examining various designs, identifying industry trends, and noting best practices. The insights gained from this research informed my design decisions, ensuring that the new website would be competitive and user-friendly.

  • Wireframing: With a clear understanding of the requirements and industry standards, I proceeded to create wireframes. These wireframes served as the blueprint for the website, outlining the structure, layout, and key elements. This step was essential for visualizing the user journey and ensuring a logical flow of information.

  • Design Execution: Utilizing Figma, I transformed the wireframes into a detailed and interactive design. I focused on creating a visually appealing interface that was both functional and engaging. Throughout the design process, I iterated based on feedback from Zidio, refining the design to meet both user needs and business goals.

Project Description

I utilized Figma to develop high-fidelity wireframes, prototypes, and final UI designs. My design process included:

  • Created user personas, journey maps, and wireframes to establish the website's structure and flow.
  • Developed interactive prototypes to demonstrate the website's functionality and user interactions.
  • Designed visually appealing UI components, ensuring consistency and adherence to Zidio's brand guidelines.
  • Delivered a final design that improved user experience and met the company's goals for the e-commerce platform.

MY APPROACH

I tackled the E-commerce website design project using a systematic and organized approach:

  • Target Analysis: I began by thoroughly analyzing the project targets and requirements set by Zidio. Understanding the company's objectives and the specific goals for the e-commerce platform was crucial in laying the foundation for the design process.

  • Market Research: Following the initial analysis, I conducted comprehensive research on existing e-commerce websites. This involved studying competitors, identifying best practices, and understanding current trends in the industry. The insights gained from this research were invaluable in informing the design strategy and ensuring the final product was both innovative and user-centric.

  • Wireframing: With a clear understanding of the project goals and market landscape, I proceeded to create wireframes. These wireframes served as blueprints for the website, outlining the structure, layout, and key functionalities. This step was essential in visualizing the user journey and ensuring a seamless flow throughout the site.

  • Design Execution: Utilizing Figma, I transformed the wireframes into a detailed and interactive design. I focused on creating a visually appealing interface that was both functional and engaging. Throughout the design process, I iterated based on feedback from Zidio, refining the design to meet both user needs and business goals.
